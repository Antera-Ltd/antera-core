# ANTERA Ventures: Disruptive Platform Roadmap

We don't build features; we build ecosystems. Inspired by the hyper-scalability of global platforms like Glovo, Uber, and Grab, ANTERA is developing a suite of "everything-as-a-service" platforms tailored for the Pan-African digital economy, starting with Tanzania.

---

## 1. **Kilele (The "Everything" Delivery App)**
**Thesis:** Hyper-local logistics is the backbone of African commerce. If you can move it, you can monetize it.
- **Problem:** Fragmented delivery services, high "trust deficit" in informal couriers, and no real-time tracking for the middle class.
- **Solution:** A multi-vertical platform connecting users with riders for food, groceries, pharmacy, and "anything" courier tasks.
- **Revenue Engine:** 15-30% merchant commission + Dynamic delivery fees + "Kilele Prime" subscription for unlimited free delivery.

## 2. **DukaOS (The Operating System for Retail)**
**Thesis:** The future of retail isn't just selling; it's data-driven inventory and embedded finance.
- **Problem:** Millions of Dukas have zero digital footprint, making them invisible to credit providers and FMCG brands.
- **Solution:** A lightweight tablet/mobile OS that handles POS, inventory, and automated restocking.
- **Revenue Engine:** SaaS subscription + 1% payment processing fee + Data monetization fees from FMCG brands for "shelf-space" intelligence.

## 3. **BodaCore (Fleet Intelligence & Asset Finance)**
**Thesis:** Turn the motorcycle into a smart asset, not just a vehicle.
- **Problem:** Boda riders are unbanked and lack the data to prove their creditworthiness for motorcycle ownership.
- **Solution:** IoT-enabled tracking and safety monitoring that builds a "driver score" for lease-to-own financing.
- **Revenue Engine:** Hardware/SaaS lease + Financing interest + B2B API access for logistics companies.

## 4. **ShambaLogic (Agritech Supply Chain Platform)**
**Thesis:** Control the supply, control the market.
- **Problem:** Massive post-harvest loss due to lack of cold-chain and direct-to-consumer pipelines.
- **Solution:** A platform connecting commercial farms directly to urban consumption hubs (hotels, schools, supermarkets) with integrated logistics.
- **Revenue Engine:** 10% marketplace commission + Logistics fulfillment fees + Quality certification fees.

## 5. **AfyaFlow (Digital Health Marketplace)**
**Thesis:** Healthcare access should be as easy as ordering a pizza.
- **Problem:** Difficulty finding specialists, lack of portable medical records, and fragmented drug availability.
- **Solution:** A consumer-facing app for tele-consultations, home lab-tests, and pharmacy delivery.
- **Revenue Engine:** Commission on doctor fees + Markup on pharmacy deliveries + B2B SaaS for clinics.

## 6. **Elimu360 (On-Demand Learning Ecosystem)**
**Thesis:** Professional education and skills-matching are the keys to the youth bulge.
- **Problem:** Education-to-employment gap; university degrees don't guarantee technical skills.
- **Solution:** A micro-learning platform paired with a "Jobs Marketplace" that guarantees interviews for certified graduates.
- **Revenue Engine:** Course fees + Corporate recruitment fees + Subscription for continuous professional development.

## 7. **Makazi (Smart Property & Rent Finance)**
**Thesis:** Decouple housing access from the "one-year-advance" rent trap.
- **Problem:** Tanzanian landlords demand 6-12 months of rent upfront, locking out the workforce.
- **Solution:** A platform that vets tenants and pays landlords upfront, while tenants pay monthly (Rent-Now-Pay-Later).
- **Revenue Engine:** 10% convenience fee on monthly rent + Property management SaaS fees.

## 8. **KaziHub (Gig Economy Middleware)**
**Thesis:** Standardize the informal service economy (Plumbers, Electricians, Cleaners).
- **Problem:** Extreme difficulty finding "verified" and "reliable" manual labor.
- **Solution:** A blue-collar marketplace with background checks, standard pricing, and a rating system.
- **Revenue Engine:** 20% platform fee + Premium placement for high-rated pros + Insurance add-ons.

## 9. **LipaCredit (B2B Buy-Now-Pay-Later)**
**Thesis:** Liquidity is the biggest bottleneck for SME growth.
- **Problem:** Small businesses can't buy stock in bulk because they lack working capital.
- **Solution:** A "Buy-Stock-Pay-Later" platform integrated into the supplier's checkout.
- **Revenue Engine:** Merchant discount rate (MDR) + Late fees + Factoring fees.

## 10. **MzigoX (Digital Freight Forwarding)**
**Thesis:** The port of Dar es Salaam is a gold mine waiting for transparency.
- **Problem:** Logistics opacity costs the region billions in lost time and demurrage.
- **Solution:** A dashboard for importers to track every step from high-seas to final-mile clearance.
- **Revenue Engine:** Per-shipment tracking fee + Customs clearance service fees + Freight insurance commissions.
